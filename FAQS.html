Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our flowers, delivery, and services. We're here to help your floral dreams blossom.

Ordering & Delivery Flower Care Payment & Policies Events & Weddings

Ordering & Delivery

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website, by phone, or by visiting our store. Online ordering is available 24/7 for your convenience. We recommend placing orders at least 48 hours in advance for special occasions.

What are your delivery areas and fees?

We deliver within a 25-mile radius of our store. Delivery fees start at $6.99 and may vary based on distance and delivery time. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM. We also offer express delivery for an additional fee.

Can I schedule a delivery for a specific date?

Yes, you can schedule deliveries for future dates during checkout. We recommend ordering at least 48 hours in advance for specific date requests, especially during holidays. For weddings and large events, we suggest placing orders 2-3 weeks in advance.

What if no one is home during delivery?

Our delivery drivers will attempt to contact the recipient. If no one is available, they will leave the flowers in a safe, shaded location and leave a delivery notification. In extreme weather conditions, we may hold the delivery and contact you to reschedule.

Do you offer international delivery?

Currently, we only deliver within the United States. However, we partner with international florists for overseas deliveries. Please contact our customer service for assistance with international orders.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placement. After that time, orders enter our processing system and changes may not be possible. For urgent modifications, please contact our customer service team immediately.

What is your delivery time window?

Our standard delivery hours are between 9 AM and 6 PM, Monday through Saturday. For specific time requests, we offer timed delivery for an additional fee. Sunday deliveries are available for an extra charge.

Flower Care & Quality

How long will my flowers last?

With proper care, most of our floral arrangements will last 5-7 days. Some varieties may last longer. Tropical flowers can last up to 10-14 days. We include detailed care instructions with every delivery to help maximize the lifespan of your flowers.

How should I care for my flowers?

Keep flowers in a cool area away from direct sunlight, heat sources, and fruit. Change the water every 2 days and trim the stems at an angle. Remove any leaves that fall below the waterline to prevent bacterial growth. Use the flower food provided for best results.

Do you guarantee the quality of your flowers?

Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your flowers, contact us within 24 hours of delivery, and we'll make it right. We source our flowers from the finest growers and handle them with care to ensure maximum freshness and longevity.

What should I do if my flowers arrive damaged?

If your flowers arrive damaged, please contact us immediately with photos of the issue. We will either replace the arrangement or provide a full refund, depending on your preference. Our customer service team is available 7 days a week to assist you.

Are your flowers locally sourced?

We source approximately 60% of our flowers from local growers during peak seasons. The remainder comes from trusted international suppliers to ensure we can offer a wide variety of flowers year-round. All our suppliers follow sustainable farming practices.

Do you offer allergy-friendly flower options?

Yes, we offer several low-pollen and hypoallergenic flower options. Popular choices include orchids, roses, tulips, and peonies. When ordering, you can specify any allergy concerns in the special instructions, and we'll accommodate your needs.

How do I revive wilted flowers?

For mildly wilted flowers, try recutting the stems and placing them in warm water for an hour. For more severe wilting, submerge the entire flower in cool water for 30 minutes before recutting stems and placing in fresh water with flower food.

Payment & Policies

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer financing options through Affirm for larger purchases. For corporate accounts, we can set up billing with net-30 terms upon credit approval.

What is your return policy?

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we don't accept returns on floral arrangements. However, if you're unsatisfied with your order, please contact us within 24 hours, and we'll work to resolve the issue. This may include replacement, credit, or refund depending on the situation.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for weddings, corporate events, and large orders. Please contact our sales team for custom quotes on bulk orders. We also have special pricing for nonprofit organizations and repeat customers.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes! Our Bloom Rewards program offers points for every purchase that can be redeemed for discounts, free delivery, and special gifts. Members also receive early access to sales and exclusive floral varieties.

What is your policy on substitutions?

Occasionally, we may need to substitute flowers due to seasonal availability or quality issues. We always ensure substitutions are of equal or greater value and maintain the overall style and color scheme of your arrangement. We'll notify you of any significant substitutions before delivery.

Do you offer gift wrapping and custom messages?

Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping and can include a custom message card with your order. You can add these options during checkout. For special occasions, we also offer premium gift wrapping for an additional fee.

What is your privacy policy?

We value your privacy and never share your personal information with third parties. Your data is securely stored and used only for order processing and communication regarding your purchases. You can view our full privacy policy on our website.

Events & Weddings

Do you provide flowers for weddings?

Absolutely! We specialize in wedding floral arrangements including bouquets, centerpieces, ceremony decorations, and more. We recommend booking wedding services at least 3-6 months in advance to ensure availability.

Can I schedule a consultation for my event?

Yes, we offer complimentary consultations for weddings and large events. During the consultation, we'll discuss your vision, budget, and preferences to create a customized floral plan. Consultations can be scheduled in-person or virtually.

Do you offer delivery and setup for events?

Yes, we provide delivery and professional setup for all event orders. Our team will work with your venue coordinator to ensure everything is placed perfectly according to your plan. Setup fees vary based on the complexity of the arrangements.

Can you work within my budget?

We pride ourselves on working with various budgets. During your consultation, we'll discuss your budget and suggest floral options that maximize impact while staying within your price range. We offer seasonal and locally-sourced flowers as cost-effective alternatives.
